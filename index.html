- About Me -

Pierfrancesco Pippo Andresini

-2D Artist / Designer-

Hello there! My name is Pierfrancesco, but everybody calls me Pippo: it's shorter and pretty handy.
Some pepole compare me to a Griphon: "head of a Designer, hand of an Artist and heart of crippled pirate."
Doc. Steve told me I suffer from C.P.A.A. (Chronic Pixel Art Addiction), I'm taking some 2D Painting Pills and other stuffs like that to get better.
My objective is to become an Art Director, well the road is long and tortuous, but I've already taken my walking stick and I'm not going to decelerate.
