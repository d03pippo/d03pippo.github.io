About Me

Pierfrancesco Pippo Andresini

- 2D Artist / Designer -

Hello there! My name is Pierfrancesco, but everybody calls me Pippo: it's short, nice and pretty handy. I was born on the summer solstice of the 1997, in Turin, Italy, where I live.
Here you can find a little warning label about me:

  -   Pepole think I'm a strange kind of mythological creature: "head of a Designer, hand of an Artist and heart of a crippled pirate."
  -   The doctor told me I suffer from C.P.A.A. (Chronic Pixel Art Addiction), but don't worry: in order to get better I'm taking some painting pills, artist drugs and so on.
  -   My objective is to become an Art Director. Well, the road is long and tortuous, but I've already taken my walking stick and I'm not going to decelerate: I'm pretty stubborn.
